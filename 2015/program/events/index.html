Pre-Conference Drinks (Open Event)

Salettl im alten AKH — Day #0: Sunday, July 19th

For Pre-Conference Drinks on Sunday, July 19th, we are going to meet up at Salettl directly after Pre-Registration closes at 18:00. Meet with other attendees for some casual get-to-know-each other drinks, food and nice chats!

Please note this is a "Cash Only" place, though there are cash points nearby!

The venue for this get-together is right next to the conference venue, less than a minute walk from our registration. It's within the first courtyard of the campus (Hof 1):

Official Party (Open Event)

Stiegl Ambulanz — Day #1: Monday, July 20th

Join us on Monday, July 20th for the nucl.ai 2015 Official Party, to be held at the Stiegl Ambulanz. It takes place right after the last session if you're an attendee, or at 18:30 if you're a local developer. The restaurant is a great place for both food and drinks, so expect to spend hours chatting with fellow professionals and enthusiasts!

The venue for the party is right next to the conference venue, less than a minute walk from the amphitheatres. It's within the first courtyard of the campus (Hof 1):

  Altes AKH

  Uni Campus, Hof 1

  Alserstrasse 4

  1090 Wien

See you on Monday. Prost!

V.I.P. Dinner (Invitation Only, Sold Out!)

Levante Parliament — Day #2: Tuesday, July 21st

For All Access ticket holders and conference speakers only, meet us on Tuesday, July 21st for the nucl.ai 2015 V.I.P. Dinner, to be held at The Levante Parliament hotel. The evening starts shortly after the last session; dinner and one drink is included—but don't forget your badge and and ticket that you received during registration. (This event is sold out, there are no +1s possible.)

The venue for the V.I.P. Dinner is a short walk away from the conference venue. You can easily make your way directly with the map, or join the groups of people leaving after the last session. Here's the official address:

  Auerspergstraße 9

  1080 Wien

See you on Tuesday. Guten Appetit!
