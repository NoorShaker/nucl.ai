Conference Tracks

The nucl.ai Conference is made up of (the equivalent of) nine day-long courses on specific topics, with three of these tracks running in parallel each day over three days. The current topics are as follows:

{% include tracks/header.html %}

Each of these specialized tracks consists of an inspiring keynote and/or presentations from high-profile applications, specialized masterclasses and in-depth tutorials, panel discussions and expert Q&A, and (if applicable) an open laboratory in workshop-style.

The different types of sessions are scheduled in different rooms throughout the day, so you're free to follow an entire track all day or you can stay in a specific room for a different track if you enjoy the format!
